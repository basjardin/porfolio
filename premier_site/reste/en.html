our story:

The ice cream truck story began in 1990 when my father wanted to start selling homemade ice cream near the Cathedral de Notre Dame in Rouen. Due to a lack of customers, my father had to stop the business. However, in 2017, I decided to revive my father's dream by offering additional services such as pastries and lottery.

The manufacture of our products:

we use organic ingredient, made in France, close to rouen to offer you quality products. The production of our pastries and ice cream are home-made, with no added gluten, coloring or artificial flavors

our products:

The ice truck is the only producer of pastries and ice cream of all Normandy, one of France’s producers not to miss, come eat our delicious ice cream which has up to 4 different flavors, if you don’t like ice cream then eat our delicious pastries like our cookies, our croissant or our waffle, all homemade

the location:

the location of our Ice truck is located in the center of Rouen, on the rue du change, just to the south of the beautiful cathedral Notre Dame de Rouen.
